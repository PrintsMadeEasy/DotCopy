Uploading Images

All of the images that you plan on using within your Variable Data project must be uploaded onto the server. We provide a tool that will allow you to upload many images at once. The software is written in Active X and Java. You may get a warning message that the program must be installed on your computer... just select Yes.

If you plan on uploading thousands of images we recommend doing it in batches of a couple hundred or so. In case you lose your Internet connection or something it will be easier to deal with the problem.

No single image can exceed 1 MB and the only formats accepted are PNG, GIF, JPEG, TIFF, and BMP. If your artwork is in another artwork format you must convert it.

In case of a corrupt image or a file size that is too large, our uploading program will silently skip over it. You will not get any warning or error message that a particular image did not upload correctly. However you can launch a pop-up window to show you what images have been successfully imported. This list may be useful for troubleshooting purposes and you can even see a preview of each image.

Try to get your images re-sized at the proper DPI before uploading them to our server. Sometimes people will upload large image files and it may slow down the process significantly if our server has to re-size and process a very large quantity.
