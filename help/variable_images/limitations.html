Limitations

Generally there is a way to accomplish whatever you need to be done. Below are some of the things to be aware of when designing a variable image project.

1) Variable Images will be placed (layered) on top of standard text layers within your artwork. If you need text (from the editing tool) to sit on top of a variable image, you will need to put a variable within that text layer. Even if you substitute the variable with a blank space, that fact that the text layer has a variable in it will force it to lay on top of a variable image.

2) Variable Images do not work well if they are meant to "bleed" over the edge of the design. If you have a special requirement for this, please save your work and let us know.

3) If your variable images contain an alpha channel (such as a PNG or GIF with transparency), the transparency will be lost.
